About Me

Interests & Extracurricular Activities

Bouldering & Rock Climbing

I started bouldering in early 2015. Bouldering has become a really important part of my life. It helped me learn how to have fun with physical activity, which helped me lose a lot of weight and become much more confident about my body image. Now I frequent climbing gyms in Toronto and Waterloo whenever I get the chance.

Board Games

I am a large collector of board games. When my high school friends and I started watching TableTop, we immediately started doing board game nights any time we could. Now, I frequently visit gaming nights held by the university's mathematics society, as well as events held by groups on Meetup.com when I'm in Toronto.

Music

Over the years, music has become one of the most important parts of my life. I try my best to listen to and appreciate every genre equally, but I often lean towards classic rock and grunge. Live music has become a staple in my life, as well. Some of my favourite shows have been Foo Fighters, which I saw in 2015 during the Broken Leg Tour, and Rush, who I watched during their 2010 Time Machine tour.
