Interests & Extracurricular Activities

Bouldering & Rock Climbing

Bouldering has become a really important part of my life. It helped me learn how to have fun with physical activity, which helped me lose a lot of weight in early 2015. Now I frequent climbing gyms in Toronto and Waterloo whenever I get the chance.

Board Games

I am a large collector of board games. When my high school friends and I started watching TableTop, we immediately started doing board game nights any time we could.

Music

Over the years, music has become one of the most important parts of my life. Some of the best nights of my life have been spent attending concerts from Foo Fighters and Rush.
